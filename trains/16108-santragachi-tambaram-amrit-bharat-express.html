16108 | Santragachi - Tambaram Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 16108 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects Santragachi Junction (SRC) in West Bengal to Tambaram (TBM) in Tamil Nadu, passing through states of West Bengal, Odisha, Andhra Pradesh & Tamil Nadu. It covers a total 1670 kilometres over a duration of approximately 33 hours 10 Minutes.

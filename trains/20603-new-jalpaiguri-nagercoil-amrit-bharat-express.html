20603 | New Jalpaiguri - Nagercoil Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 20603 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects New Jalpaiguri (NJP) in West Bengal to the southernmost tip of India Nagercoil Junction (NCJ) in Tamil Nadu, passing through states of West Bengal, Bihar, Odisha, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu & Kerala. It covers a total 3,102 kilometres over a duration of approximately 54 hours 15 minutes.

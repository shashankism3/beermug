15671 | Kamakhya – Rohtak Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 15671 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects Kamakhya (KYQ) in Assam to Rohtak Jn (ROK) in Haryana, passing through states of Assam, West Bengal, Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi & Haryana. It covers a total 1,939 kilometres over a duration of approximately 40 hours 45 Minutes.

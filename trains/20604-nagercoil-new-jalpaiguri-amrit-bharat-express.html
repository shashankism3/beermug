20604 | Nagercoil - New Jalpaiguri Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 20604 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects the southernmost tip of India Nagercoil Junction (NCJ) in Tamil Nadu to New Jalpaiguri (NJP) in West Bengal, passing through states of Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Odisha, Bihar & West Bengal. It covers a total 3,122 kilometres over a duration of approximately 54 hours 00 minutes.

15949 | Dibrugarh – Gomti Nagar Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 15949 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects Dibrugarh (DBRG) in Assam to Gomti Nagar (GTNR), Lucknow in Uttar Pradesh, passing through states of Assam, Nagaland, West Bengal, Bihar & Uttar Pradesh. It covers a total 1,895 kilometres over a duration of approximately 40 hours 30 Minutes.

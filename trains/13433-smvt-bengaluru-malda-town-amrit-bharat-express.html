13433 | SMVT Bengaluru - Malda Town Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 13433 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects Bengaluru (SMVB) in Karnataka to Malda Town (MLDT) in West Bengal passing through Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Odisha, Jharkhand & West Bengal. It covers a total distance of 2,264 km in approximately 43 hours and 25 minutes.

11015 | Mumbai LTT - Saharsa Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 11015 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects Mumbai Lokmanya Tilak Terminus (LTT) in Maharashtra to Saharsa Junction (SHC) in Bihar, passing through Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh & Bihar. It covers a total 1,956 kilometres over a duration of approximately 38 hours 10 Minutes.

  • Inauguration/Launch Date: 24 April 2025
  • Origin: Mumbai Lokmanya Tilak Terminus (LTT) | Departure: 11:50 AM (Friday)
  • Destination: Saharsa Junction (SHC) | Arrival: 02:00 AM (Sunday)
  • Frequency: Weekly (Friday)
  • Total Distance: 1,956 kilometres
  • All Stations: Lokmanyatilak T, Thane, Kalyan Jn, Nashik Road, Jalgaon Jn, Bhusaval Jn, Itarsi Jn, Jabalpur, Satna, Manikpur, Prayagrajcheoki, Mirzapur, Dd Upadhyaya Jn, Buxar, Ara Jn, Danapur, Patliputra, Sonpur Jn, Hajipur Jn, Muzaffarpur Jn, Samastipur Jn, Hasanpur Road J, Salauna, Khagaria Jn, Saharsa Jn

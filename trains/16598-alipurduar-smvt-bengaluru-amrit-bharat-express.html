16598 | Alipurduar - SMVT Bengaluru Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 16598 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects Alipurduar Junction (APDJ) in West Bengal to SMVT Bengaluru (SMVB) in Karnataka, passing through states of West Bengal, Bihar, Odisha, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu & Karnataka. It covers a total 2,670 kilometres over a duration of approximately 52 hours 35 minutes.

15950 | Gomti Nagar – Dibrugarh Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 15950 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects Gomti Nagar (GTNR), Lucknow in Uttar Pradesh to Dibrugarh (DBRG) in Assam, passing through states of Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, West Bengal, Nagaland & Assam. It covers a total 1,895 kilometres over a duration of approximately 42 hours 00 Minutes.

13434 | Malda Town - SMVT Bengaluru Amrit Bharat Express Train

The 13434 Amrit Bharat Express is a weekly, high-speed, fully non-AC and push-pull train which connects Malda Town (MLDT) in West Bengal to SMVT Bengaluru (SMVB) in Karnataka passing through West Bengal, Jharkhand, Odisha, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu & Karnataka. It covers a total distance of 2,264 km in approximately 42 hours.

  • Inauguration/Launch Date: 30 December 2023
  • Origin: Malda Town (MLDT) | Departure: 08:50 AM (Sunday)
  • Destination: SMVT Bengaluru (SMVB) | Arrival: 03:00 AM (Tuesday)
  • Frequency: Weekly (Sunday)
  • Total Distance: 2,264 kilometres
